One of the most beautiful atractions that Venice has to offer is the 'Carnevale di Venezia', is one the celebrations that congregate thousands of tourist with the magic of the masks and the amazing culture of the locals.

The origin of the Carnival in Venice dates back to the XI century when to escape from the control of the government and be free for some days, people used masks to be equals with everyone, It starts in February and all the streets of Venice are filled with colors and costumes.
